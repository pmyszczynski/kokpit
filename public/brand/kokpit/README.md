# Kokpit brand assets

Use `kokpit-mark-detailed.svg` at 64 px and above, `kokpit-mark-navbar.svg` from 24 px to 48 px, and `kokpit-favicon.svg` from 16 px to 32 px. Do not substitute one for another.

## Inventory

- `kokpit-logo-dark.svg` — full lockup on an Ink field for dark/OLED product surfaces.
- `kokpit-logo-light.svg` — full lockup on a Paper field for light surfaces.
- `kokpit-mark-detailed.svg` — launcher and larger-card cockpit mark.
- `kokpit-mark-navbar.svg` — simplified navigation mark.
- `kokpit-favicon.svg` and `favicon.ico` — independently drawn micro-mark for browser tabs.
- `png/` — exact-size raster exports for web manifests and platform icons.

All artwork is self-contained vector geometry with no font or image dependency. The logo palette is fixed and deliberately independent of Kokpit's configurable `--color-accent`.
